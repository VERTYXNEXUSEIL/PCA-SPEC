# Procurement Language (Product-Neutral)

## Baseline Clause
The supplier SHALL provide a deterministic verification workflow aligned with PCA/CAE v1, including Plan Certificate digest checks and mandatory fallback behavior.

## Bronze Requirement
- Demonstrate conformance with profile baseline controls.
- Provide test evidence for required vectors in PCA-TEST v1.

## Silver Requirement
- Meet Bronze requirements.
- Demonstrate Merkle proof capability and conflict handling.

## Gold Requirement
- Meet Silver requirements.
- Demonstrate OOD tripwire and disclosure-budget enforcement.
- Provide signed release artifacts and reproducible verification evidence.
